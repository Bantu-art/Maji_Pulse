Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers. Check out our FAQ section to find the information you need quickly and easily.

What is the purpose of our smart water management system?

Our system is designed to efficiently manage water resources through real-time monitoring of water usage and leakage detection, ensuring equitable distribution and providing transparent billing information.

How do I track my water usage?

Once logged into your account, you can view detailed reports on your water usage in the dashboard. Our system uses sensors to collect data and present it in an easy-to-understand format.

How is water distribution managed?

Our platform uses blockchain technology to ensure fair and transparent water distribution. Sensors monitor water flow and usage, ensuring that resources are allocated equitably among users.

Can I view my billing details?

Yes, you can view detailed billing information directly from your user dashboard. Our system provides a clear breakdown of charges based on your water usage.

How do I report a problem or give feedback?

If you encounter any issues or have feedback, you can use our contact form to get in touch with us. We are committed to resolving any concerns and appreciate your input to improve our service.

Is my data secure with your platform?

Absolutely. We use advanced security measures to protect your data. Our blockchain technology ensures data integrity and privacy, keeping your information safe and secure.
